Conducted comprehensive data analysis on two business cases:
1. Walmart Sales Forecasting: Analyzed 2.5 years (6,435 records) of weekly sales data to identify seasonal patterns and external factor correlations
2. Online Retail Customer Analysis: Processed 387,961 transactions to derive customer behavior insights and product performance metrics

🔍 Key Activities:
- Performed extensive data preprocessing and cleaning (reduced online retail dataset from 301,296 to 211,567 valid transactions)
- Conducted time-series analysis to identify seasonal sales patterns
- Analyzed correlations between sales and external factors (temperature, fuel price, CPI, unemployment)
- Identified top-selling products and customer segments
- Evaluated holiday vs. non-holiday sales performance

📈 Key Insights:
- Holiday weeks consistently outperform non-holiday periods in sales
- Identified 3,226 unique customers with average order value of $400.67
- Discovered seasonal patterns enabling better inventory management
- Unemployment showed strongest negative correlation with sales

🛠️ Tools Used: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebook
